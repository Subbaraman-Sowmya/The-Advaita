# Advaita Repository: The Core of Realization

This repository serves as a record of insights into the essence of Advaita (Non-Duality), stripped of external scriptures and focused on the direct "processing" of awareness.

## Core Concepts

### 1. The Not-Null Void
- **Definition:** A state of contentless awareness observed when external sensory input is minimized (e.g., closing one's eyes).
- **Nature:** It is not an empty "null" but a vibrant field of potential. It is the "source code" of existence.
- **Function:** It acts as the permanent background for all manifest reality.

### 2. Manifest vs. Unmanifest
- **Unmanifest:** The eternal, potential state of existence (The Void).
- **Manifest:** The world of perception, which is an expression of the unmanifest.
- **Realization:** Existence is continuous; birth and death are merely shifts between these two states.

### 3. Non-Dual Processing
- At the deepest level, there is no distinction between the "observer" and the "observed."
- All perceived objects are projections of one's own perception within the field of awareness.

## Usage
This repository is intended for those seeking a direct, experiential understanding of non-duality through the lens of internal observation and core processing concepts.
