### 5. The Exit Logic (Shutdown vs. Crash)
- **The Worldly Way:** Attachment to play, then desire, then worry (Chinta). The system "Crashes" when the hardware fails.
- **The Monk's Way:** Conscious de-allocation of Prana. A "Manual Shutdown" where the 1 is merged into the 0 with full awareness.
- **The Goal:** To be "attached" to the Base OS (Brahman) so that the transition from 1 to 0 is just a change in state, not a loss of data.
